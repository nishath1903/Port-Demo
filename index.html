About Me

I'm Mohamed Nishath, a passionate and purpose-driven individual with a background in BCA and a growing skillset in full stack web development, content writing, and digital innovation. I’m currently undergoing hands-on training through OJT programs and platforms like NxtWave CCBP. Beyond academics, I’m a self-starter who believes in learning by doing and building with impact. As the founder of MMJ Tech Service, I launched this initiative to empower students and beginners with practical tech skills through real-world projects, mentorship, and short-term courses. MMJ Tech Service focuses on web development, digital tools, and skill-based learning programs to make tech education more accessible and job-ready. In parallel, I established the MMJ Charitable Trust, a community-focused initiative rooted in my family values and inspired by my grandfather's legacy. This trust works on small-scale but impactful missions like food distribution, education aid, and kindness-driven campaigns aimed at supporting underserved communities. Together, both MMJ Tech and MMJ Charitable Trust reflect my dual mission: to build a stronger digital future and to uplift lives through compassion. I believe in using creativity, collaboration, and consistency to solve real-world problems – one step at a time.
